Using Youtube

Using a Youtube on a Webpage

Youtube Video ID:

  1. Youtube will display an ID (like tgbNymZ7vqY), when you save (or play) a video.

  2. You can use this ID, and refer to your video in the HTML code.

To Play Your Video on a Webpage, Do the Following:

  1. Upload the video to youtube

  2. Take not of the video ID

  3. Define an iframe element in your webpage
  4. Let the src attribute point to the video URL

  5. Use the width and height attribut to specify the dimension of the player

  6. Add any other parameters to the URL

  7. Add mute=1 after autoplay=1 to let your video start playing automatically (but muted).

  8. Add controls=0 to not display controls in the video player
